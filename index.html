What are MOBAs?

MOBAs (Multiplayer Online Battle Arena) are a popular genre of video games where players control characters and fight against other teams on a map. The goal is typically to destroy the enemy's base while defending your own. MOBAs require strategic thinking, teamwork, and quick decision-making.
